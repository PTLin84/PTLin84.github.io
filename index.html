About

Kyle Lin is a Battery Data Analyst at Element Energy, where he applies his multidisciplinary expertise in Computer Science and Materials Science to solve complex challenges in the energy sector.


His work encompasses data analysis, data engineering, and the development of software and algorithms, all aimed at advancing next-generation battery management systems on a GWh scale. Kyle's mission is to leverage Machine Learning and AI to optimize battery performance while minimizing safety risks.


Kyle earned a Master’s degree in Materials Science from Stanford University, where he conducted research on solid-state batteries under Professor William Chueh. During his time at Stanford, he also developed strong skills in programming and software development, further enhancing his ability to innovate at the intersection of materials science and technology.
